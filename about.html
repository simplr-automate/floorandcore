About Floor & Core

Personalized pelvic floor physical therapy to help you thrive

What is Floor and Core?

We provide personalized one-on-one pelvic floor physical therapy and group fitness tailored to your unique needs. Based in Hoboken, NJ, we also offer in-home sessions and virtual appointments for your convenience.

Our Mission

EveryBODY has a pelvic floor — it's the foundation of our body, providing essential support for urinary, bowel, and sexual function. Over a lifetime, the pelvic floor can be impacted by pregnancy, postpartum changes, stress, and habitual patterns, often disrupting daily life. At Floor & Core, we take a science-based, whole-person approach — including the all-important core — to identify and address the root causes of dysfunction and help you return to the activities you love without fear.

Who is Gabby?

Gabby Majcher is a board-certified Doctor of Physical Therapy with advanced training in pelvic health through the American Physical Therapy Association. Having personally experienced pelvic floor challenges that were often dismissed or normalized, she understands firsthand what it's like to be in the patient perspective. Her mission is to break the taboo and start the conversation — because everyone deserves to talk about their pelvic floor health.
